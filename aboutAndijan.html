About Andijan

Andijan is the birthplace of bBabur, a well-known figure in Uzbek poetry, the founder of the Mongol dynasty, a commander and ruler. Once located on the Great Silk Road, it is a city rich in archeological monuments and historical monuments.

Population:

2981,9 thousand people

Population density:

703,3 person/km2

Territory:

4240 km2

Gas supply

1 cubic metre - 1000 UZS

Power supply

1 kWh - 365,76 UZS

Water supply

1 cubic metre - 445 UZS Drinking water and sewage

Land resources

The total land area is 26.1 thousand hectares. Of these, irrigated agricultural area is 23.4 thousand hectares, non-irrigated agricultural area is 3.1 thousand hectares, and pasture area for livestock is 3 thousand hectares.

Mineral resources

There are many well-known oil resources in Andijan region. At the moment, the districts of Andijan region have 8 large and 2 small mines. There are 2 artificial and 1 large reservoir in the region
